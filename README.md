# BlackCrest Platform

Industrial Intelligence for Modern Operators.

BlackCrest is a modular industrial intelligence ecosystem built for manufacturers, procurement teams, sourcing leaders, distributors, aerospace suppliers, operational decision makers, and small businesses trying to compete for larger opportunities.

The platform combines procurement intelligence, supplier visibility, operational analytics, funding access, and enterprise AI governance into one scalable operational environment.

## Funding Marketplace Vision
Small companies do not only need help finding opportunities. They need help getting the money, working capital, equipment, payroll float, bonding support, and financing relationships required to actually pursue and execute those opportunities.

BlackCrest Funding Marketplace is designed to connect small businesses with capital pathways and funding partners based on the opportunity they are pursuing, their operational readiness, their documentation, and their execution profile.

The goal is simple: give smaller businesses a practical avenue to capital so they can compete with larger companies instead of being boxed out before the fight even starts.

See `docs/funding-access-module.md` for the initial product spec.

## Security & Governance Philosophy
- Human-in-the-loop operational decision support
- Governance-first architecture
- Customer-controlled data ingestion
- Read-only operational intelligence posture
- Designed for non-classified operational environments

## Compliance Boundary
BlackCrest Funding Marketplace is intended to provide business intelligence, funding-readiness workflow support, and marketplace introductions. It is not positioned as a lender, financial advisor, securities advisor, or guaranteed funding source unless proper legal, licensing, and partner compliance work is completed.
