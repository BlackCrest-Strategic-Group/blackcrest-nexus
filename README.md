# BlackCrest Procurement Intelligence Operating System

BlackCrest AI is a Procurement Intelligence Operating System designed for commercial procurement, sourcing, supplier management, ERP intelligence, operational analytics, and enterprise procurement visibility.

The platform is evolving toward a broader commercial procurement intelligence ecosystem suitable for manufacturing, logistics, distribution, industrial operations, hospitality, retail, healthcare, aerospace, and enterprise procurement environments globally.

## Core capabilities
- Procurement intelligence dashboards
- Supplier intelligence and supplier scoring
- Spend visibility and procurement analytics
- Margin leak detection and operational risk monitoring
- ERP connector architecture and upload ingestion workflows
- Executive narrative reporting
- Procurement workflow visibility
- Explainable AI-driven operational insights

## Commercial positioning
BlackCrest is designed to support:
- Commercial procurement teams
- Multi-site procurement operations
- ERP-driven sourcing environments
- Supplier and vendor management programs
- Procurement analytics and executive reporting

## Enterprise deployment direction
The platform now supports the foundation for:
- Containerized enterprise deployments
- Licensing-based deployment models
- White-label procurement intelligence opportunities
- Multi-tenant procurement environments
- Private customer-hosted deployment models

## ERP connector architecture
Supported providers in Connector Center:
- SAP
- Oracle
- Infor / Syteline
- Microsoft Dynamics
- CSV / SFTP export
- Manual upload

## Security philosophy
- Read-only intelligence layer
- Human-in-the-loop operational workflows
- Customer-controlled ERP integrations
- Container-ready deployment architecture
- Governance-first operational design
