How It Works

From search to scored
opportunity in seconds

A streamlined four-step process that turns raw federal data into actionable procurement intelligence.

1
Connect Your Profile
Enter your NAICS codes, set-aside status, and capability areas. BlackCrest AI learns your business profile and priorities.
2
AI Scans All Sources
Our engine simultaneously scans SAM.gov, USASpending, SBIR, Grants.gov, and commercial sources in real time.
3
Score & Prioritize
Every opportunity gets a 0–100 AI bid score based on your profile, past performance patterns, and FAR/DFARS analysis.
4
Act & Win
Receive your daily digest, upload documents for deeper analysis, and use ERP data for real-time capacity decisions.
