# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

## Overview

BlackCrest Nexus is a procurement intelligence and operational visibility platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, processed, and protected while using the platform.

---

## Information We May Collect

Depending on platform usage, the following categories of information may be processed:

- Account information
- Uploaded operational documents
- Procurement workflow data
- Supplier-related information
- Usage analytics
- Device and browser information

---

## Operational Analytics

BlackCrest Nexus may use operational analytics to improve platform functionality, system reliability, and user experience.

Analytics may include:

- Session activity
- Performance monitoring
- Platform interaction metrics
- Workflow usage statistics

---

## Cookies

The platform may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Maintain user sessions
- Improve platform functionality
- Support authentication workflows
- Analyze platform performance
- Remember user preferences

Users may manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

---

## File Uploads

Uploaded files may be processed to generate procurement intelligence insights, operational summaries, workflow recommendations, or AI-assisted analysis.

Organizations are responsible for ensuring uploaded content complies with their own internal policies and regulatory obligations.

---

## AI-Assisted Features

BlackCrest Nexus includes AI-assisted workflow and analysis capabilities.

AI-generated outputs are intended to support operational decision-making and should be reviewed by authorized personnel before business use.

---

## Security Measures

The platform is being developed with security-focused deployment practices including:

- Access control measures
- Secure deployment architecture
- Restricted infrastructure access
- Environment variable protection
- Operational monitoring

---

## Data Retention

Data retention policies may vary depending on deployment configuration and organizational requirements.

Organizations should establish their own governance and retention procedures when deploying the platform.

## Third-Party Services

BlackCrest Nexus may integrate with third-party services including:

Third-party services may maintain their own privacy policies and operational standards.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically as platform functionality evolves.
