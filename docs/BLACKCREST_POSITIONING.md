# BlackCrest AI Positioning

## Platform Identity
BlackCrest AI is a Procurement Operating System focused on procurement intelligence and operational visibility.

The platform combines:
- procurement intelligence
- supplier intelligence
- sourcing workflows
- executive visibility
- ERP analytics

into one operating environment.

## Core Positioning Statement
The AI Procurement Operating System.

## Platform Focus
BlackCrest helps procurement teams understand:
- what matters
- where risk exists
- what requires attention
- recommended next actions

## Target Industries
- Government contracting
- Manufacturing
- Retail
- Logistics

## Capability Areas
### Opportunity Intelligence
- RFP analysis
- compliance visibility
- proposal analysis

### Supplier Intelligence
- supplier health scoring
- delivery risk visibility
- vendor performance tracking

### Executive Intelligence
- margin leak visibility
- procurement health scoring
- spend analysis

### Workflow Intelligence
- procurement copilots
- sourcing support
- workflow summaries

## Long-Term Vision
Build a unified procurement intelligence ecosystem for enterprise procurement organizations.
