# BlackCrest Procurement Intelligence OS

## Vision
BlackCrest Procurement Intelligence OS is a modular procurement ecosystem combining:

- GovCon intelligence
- Global supplier sourcing
- RFQ workflows
- Supplier intelligence
- Category management
- Spend analytics
- AI sourcing recommendations
- Sentinel enterprise security

## Platform Modules

### GovCon Intelligence
Federal opportunity intelligence, FAR/DFARS analysis, compliance scoring, and proposal analysis.

### Global Source Exchange
Commercial supplier marketplace focused on direct and indirect procurement.

### Procurement Analytics
Spend intelligence, supplier benchmarking, KPI dashboards, and sourcing analytics.

### RFQ Marketplace
Buyer and supplier collaboration engine for sourcing events.

### Supplier Intelligence
Supplier capability profiles, risk visibility, certifications, and global sourcing intelligence.

### Category Management
Indirect and direct procurement category dashboards and commodity intelligence.

### Sentinel Enterprise
Secure enterprise procurement deployment architecture with tenant isolation and private AI.
