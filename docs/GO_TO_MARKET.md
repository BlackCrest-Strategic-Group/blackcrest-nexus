## Recommended commercial model
Use a **hybrid model**:
1. **Direct SaaS** for speed (self-serve + sales-assisted).
2. **Licensing/white-label** as a premium enterprise path.

This gives near-term revenue while preserving upside from larger licensing deals.

## Site architecture recommendation
- Keep BlackCrest as the umbrella brand site.
- Position "GovCon AI (Powered by Truth Serum)" as the flagship product.
- Keep Truth Serum as the proprietary intelligence engine, promoted across every product page and email touchpoint.

## Conversion flow
1. Traffic lands on `/` (brand story + category framing).
2. Visitors choose product track on `/blackcrest-ai`.
3. CTA drives to `/app` (trial/demo) or to sales form for enterprise licensing.

## Enterprise licensing path
Add a dedicated page `/blackcrest-ai/licensing` with:
- Deployment models (single-tenant cloud, private cloud)
- Security/compliance posture (MFA, audit logs, data boundaries)
- Commercial models (seat, site, enterprise license)
- Contact CTA for enterprise demos and pilot pricing
