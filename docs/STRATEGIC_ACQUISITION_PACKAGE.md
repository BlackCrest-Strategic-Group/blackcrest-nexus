# BlackCrest Procurement Intelligence OS - Strategic Acquisition Package

## Positioning
BlackCrest Procurement Intelligence OS is an early-stage ERP-connected procurement intelligence platform foundation for manufacturers, government contractors, distributors, sourcing teams, and operational leaders.

The platform is designed to turn ERP-style procurement data into supplier risk visibility, margin leakage detection, late delivery exposure, sourcing actions, and executive procurement intelligence.

## Valuation Thesis
The current codebase alone is not positioned as a finished enterprise SaaS company. The strategic value comes from the combination of:

- Vertical procurement intelligence positioning
- ERP-ready data model and ingestion logic
- Supplier risk and margin leakage analytics
- Governance and AI-safety layer through Sentinel
- Role-based executive/buyer workflows
- Commercial roadmap for SaaS, white-label, licensing, and implementation partnerships

Target strategic value: **up to $1.2M** depending on buyer type, included rights, exclusivity, transition support, and commercialization scope.

## Core Modules

### Procurement Intelligence
Supplier visibility, sourcing workflows, procurement dashboards, opportunity qualification, ERP-ready operational insight, and buyer-ready action support.

### Truth Serum
Operational intelligence engine for margin leakage, late delivery patterns, supplier risk, recovery opportunities, root-cause narratives, and executive recommendations.

### Sentinel
Governance layer for audit visibility, role-based access, data boundaries, AI controls, and enterprise-safe decision support.
